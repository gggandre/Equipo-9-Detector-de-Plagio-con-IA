The application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) is increasingly prevalent in various Human Resources (HR) functions. This research seeks to understand how experts from different organizations perceive artificial intelligence-based recommender systems for matching job profiles with employee profiles. Through a Delphi study-based methodology, an expert panel rates and comments on a set of propositions. Based on the online Delphi study results and participant opinions, this research identifies challenges related to employee-job profile matching through artificial intelligence and machine learning tools in the form of recommender systems. The study delves into the challenges of matching employee profiles to job profiles and the current problems faced by executives, HR personnel, or supervisors in organizations. It also explores potential solutions using artificial intelligence in the form of recommender systems, testing propositions focused on potential solutions and challenges for matching employee profiles to job profiles.
