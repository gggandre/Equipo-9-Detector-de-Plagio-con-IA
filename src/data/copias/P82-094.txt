Artificial Intelligence (AI) represents a transformative innovation deeply integrated into various aspects of daily life and industrial operations. Its rapid advancement holds the promise of revolutionizing numerous sectors, from cutting-edge industries to individual lifestyles. AI continually enhances human experiences, influencing interactions and augmenting capabilities. For instance, modern educational institutions leverage AI algorithms to track attendance using facial recognition technology. Looking ahead, the emergence of autonomous vehicles exemplifies a pinnacle of AI application, relying entirely on AI systems for navigation, traffic signal detection, and road traversal.
