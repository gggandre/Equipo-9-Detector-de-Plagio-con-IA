Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Deep Learning (DL) have ushered in a new era for advanced robotics, making robots more intelligent, efficient, and adaptable to complex tasks and environments. These technologies find applications in various domains, including autonomous navigation, object recognition, natural language processing, predictive maintenance, and collaborative robotics. AI, ML, and DL contribute to safety, efficiency, and convenience in advanced transportation systems, manufacturing assembly robots, aviation management, and taxi services. This review provides an overview of current developments in AI, ML, and DL in advanced robotics systems, discussing their applications and suggesting further research directions to enhance productivity in advanced robotic industries.
