The concept of the metaverse has emerged as a novel paradigm for human-computer interaction (HCI) and virtual collaboration, addressing existing gaps in the literature. This comprehensive review aims to elucidate the nature of the metaverse and its foundational elements from a human-centric perspective. A detailed framework encompassing the metaverse's essence, infrastructure technologies, and input/output technologies facilitating multi-sensory HCI, alongside applications across diverse domains, is presented. Insights into the metaverse's nature and the readiness level of current technologies are provided through an in-depth analysis of framework components. Major open challenges are outlined, and promising directions for future exploration and investigation are proposed. By clarifying the metaverse's vision and characterizing the building blocks necessary for its realization, this review serves as a valuable resource for metaverse developers and researchers striving to advance this transformative medium.
