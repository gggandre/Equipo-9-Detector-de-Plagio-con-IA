Human-AI interaction emerges as a focal point in fostering more responsive and humane technology, particularly with the deployment of artificial empathy strategies. This research endeavors to optimize human-AI interactions through the application of artificial empathy strategies to enhance affective and social customer experiences. Employing a qualitative research approach, this study reviews diverse studies and related literature to glean insights into the efficacy of artificial empathy strategies in human-AI interactions. The integration of technologies such as natural language processing, emotion recognition, and sentiment analysis empowers AI to respond sensitively and precisely to user emotions and needs, thereby bolstering interaction quality and customer experiences.
